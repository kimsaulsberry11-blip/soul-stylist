Step-by-step instructions for every major AI image tool — pick whichever you already use.

💬
ChatGPT (Best for photo-accurate)
Free & Plus — Recommended
  1. Go to chatgpt.com and start a new chat
  2. Click the 📎 paperclip and upload your room photo
  3. Switch to the Copy Prompt tab above and copy your prompt
  4. Paste the prompt in the chat and hit Send
  5. ChatGPT will generate your styled room using your actual photo as reference
🔥
Adobe Firefly
Free tier available — Great for interiors
  1. Go to firefly.adobe.com and sign in (free Adobe account works)
  2. Choose Generative Fill or Text to Image
  3. Upload your room photo, paste your prompt
  4. Use Structure Reference to preserve your room's layout
  5. Generate — produces highly realistic interior results
🎨
Canva AI (Magic Media)
Free with Canva account
  1. Open Canva and create a new design
  2. Click Apps → Magic Media → Text to Image
  3. Paste your copied prompt
  4. Set style to Photo or Cinematic
  5. Generate 4 variations and pick your favorite
⚡
Ideogram
Free tier — Excellent for styled rooms
  1. Go to ideogram.ai and create a free account
  2. Paste your copied prompt in the text box
  3. Select Realistic style
  4. Generate — Ideogram excels at interior design imagery
  5. Download your favorite and save it alongside your mood board
